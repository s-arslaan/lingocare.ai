Root Causes

Three problems. No single tool was solving all three.

Nursing schools across Germany struggle with the same trio: language barriers for international trainees, teachers buried in admin with no AI support, and generic content that doesn't adapt to each learner.

🌍
Language Barrier
International trainees arrive speaking A2–B1 German. Clinical vocabulary, patient communication, and care documentation demand B2+. No existing tool taught care German in context.
📚
Generic Content
Hundreds of small Pflegeschulen lack digital infrastructure. Teachers build materials from scratch. Content isn't adaptive, isn't aligned to regional curriculum, and isn't measurable.
📊
No Visibility
Directors can't see which classes are falling behind until it's too late. Teachers can't track individual learner progress. Schools fly blind until dropout statistics arrive.
