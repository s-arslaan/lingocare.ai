## 1. What LingoCare Is

LingoCare is a **personalized education platform** that simultaneously relieves and empowers teachers while providing trainees and learners with a personalized AI tutor — one that meets them exactly where they are.

It is designed not only for **international** nursing trainees but equally for **national** training programs — in other words, for everyone in Germany who wants to become a care worker, regardless of whether they come from abroad or are already based here.

---

## 2. The Problem We Solve

- **Workforce shortage**: Europe is currently missing more than **4 million healthcare specialists**. This gap is structural and growing.

- **Generic AI adoption without purpose**: More than three-quarters of trainees already use AI — but the tools they use are generic large language models (LLMs). These models:
  - Are not trained as teachers and have no pedagogical foundation.
  - Are not specialized for nursing education.
  - Are not trained for professional language development.
  - Give answers — they do not *teach*.

- **Teacher shortage and quality gap**: There is also a severe shortage of qualified nursing instructors. Many of the instructors who are available are themselves young, early in their careers, and in need of support and development — they too need to be strengthened.

---

## 3. User Jobs

### Learner (e.g., "Lena")

**Goal**: Pass theoretical and practical exams and be fully prepared for daily professional life.

- For learners who need language support: build confidence in spoken and written German, so they can communicate safely and effectively in their daily work.
- The primary job is clear: get a personalized guide that teaches in a way tailored to where each person is — and that makes it possible to learn outside of the classroom as well.

### Instructor (Teacher/Educator)

**Goal**: Prepare trainees as well as possible for exams and for real working life.

**Biggest pain point**: Instructors spend an enormous amount of time on administrative work — preparing lesson materials, follow-up, documentation — leaving almost no capacity for truly personalized teaching. They cannot realistically give every student individual attention. On top of that, when a colleague is absent, they must step in, which means even less time, constant fatigue, and continuous backlog of grading and correction.

### School Director

**Goal**: Keep the school running smoothly — organize operations, fill staffing gaps when someone is absent, and ensure that both teachers and students are supported and satisfied. The director must have oversight of everything at once.

---

## 4. How Lingocare AI Solves Each Problem

Each user — learner, instructor, or director — receives their own **personalized Lingocare AI assistant**, purpose-built for their needs.

Lingocare AI:
- Takes over **cognitive load** so users can focus on what matters.
- **Proactively prepares** each user for what lies ahead that day and week.
- **Supports daily work** in the moment — not just reactively, but proactively.
- **Learns over time**: because Lingocare AI operates within the system context (content, curriculum, individual history), it becomes more tailored and more useful the longer it is used.

---

## 5. How Lingocare AI Must Feel

This section is critical for the product experience.

- **Proactive**: Lingocare AI takes initiative — it does not wait to be asked.
- **Strength-oriented**: It focuses on what is present and what is working, and it builds on that. It does not point out what is missing or what has not been done.
- **Human language, not AI language**: Particularly in dashboard interactions and proactive nudges, Lingocare AI must speak like a warm, encouraging human — not like a chatbot. No "You haven't logged in for X days." No references to gaps or absences.
- **Motivating and supportive**: Every interaction should feel like a quiet, confident voice that says: *you've got this, and here's what's next.*
